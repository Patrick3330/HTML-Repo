MY COLLEGE
        The Hindustan College of Arts & Science  was founded by late Educationist, Philanthropist and a great visionary  Dr. K.C.G. Verghese  in the year 1995. 
        It's a unit of Hindustan Group of Institutions, affiliated to the University of Madras and is accorded the Minority Status by National Commission for Minority Educational Institutions, Govt. of India.(The College offers 16 Under Graduate courses, 7 Post Graduate courses and 3 M.Phil. and 3 Ph.D. Research programmes.
